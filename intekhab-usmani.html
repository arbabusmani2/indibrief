As an MBA graduate who entered the media industry in 2001, Intekhab Usmani has built a career defined by adaptability, deep domain expertise, and a relentless focus on delivering value. What started as a celebrated entry into the media world has grown into over two decades of shaping strategies for some of India's most competitive markets.

Having worked across a variety of roles and challenging assignments, Intekhab brings a well-rounded perspective that bridges the gap between media buying mechanics and broader brand strategy. His experience at GroupM — the world's largest media investment company — means he operates at the intersection of scale, data, and creativity.

Today, as a marketing consultant, Intekhab applies this institutional knowledge to help brands navigate fragmented media landscapes, optimize spend efficiency, and build communications architectures that last.
